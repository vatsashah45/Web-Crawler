Policy on filming and photography on University premises [PDF]        Journalists  If you are a news journalist looking to shoot an interview or video on campus, you must obtain prior permission by emailing your request to or calling 514-848-2424, ext. 4880.         Film or Video Production Crews  Filming on campus is permitted only from  the third week of June to the second week of August . Outside of that period, the University will not consider any requests. Exceptions will be considered ONLY for news journalists or current members of the Concordia community (student, faculty or staff member) who are working on an academic project.         NON-journalists  For NON-journalists to obtain permission to film on campus, please complete the fields below and submit your request at least 30 business days prior to the anticipated film shoot.
